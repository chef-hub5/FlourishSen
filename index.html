About FlourishSen

Our Story

FlourishSen is a premium fashion destination that brings you the latest trends and timeless classics. Founded with a passion for style and quality, we curate collections that help you express your unique personality.

Our mission is to make high-quality fashion accessible to everyone, offering carefully selected pieces from renowned designers and emerging talents alike.

Whether you're looking for everyday essentials or statement pieces for special occasions, FlourishSen is your trusted partner in fashion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return items purchased with Pay on Delivery?

Yes, all items can be returned regardless of payment method used.

Who pays for return shipping?

We provide prepaid return labels for defective items. For other returns, shipping costs may apply.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes! We offer exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item, subject to availability.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Contact us immediately with photos. We'll arrange a free return and full refund or replacement.

Can I return sale items?

Most sale items can be returned unless marked "Final Sale." Check the product page for specific return eligibility.

Refund Policy

Return Window

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided they are in original condition with tags attached.

Return Process

To initiate a return, contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Non-Returnable Items

Underwear, swimwear, and personalized items cannot be returned for hygiene and customization reasons.
