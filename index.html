Ordering & Shopping

How do I place an order?

Step 1: Browse our categories or use the search bar to find products

Step 2: Click on a product to view details

Step 3: Click "Add to Cart" and continue shopping or proceed to checkout

Step 4: Fill in your delivery details and choose payment method

Step 5: Complete payment and receive order confirmation

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified within 2 hours of placement. Contact our support team immediately at +234 802 301 1230 or use live chat. After 2 hours, orders enter processing and cannot be changed.

How do I use the wishlist?

Click the heart (‚ù§Ô∏è) icon on any product to add it to your wishlist. Access your wishlist anytime by clicking the heart icon in the top right corner. You can move items from wishlist to cart easily.

What if an item is out of stock?

Out of stock items will show "Notify Me" instead of "Add to Cart". Click this to get notified when the item is back in stock. We typically restock popular items within 1-2 weeks.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! Orders above ‚Ç¶200,000 qualify for bulk discounts. Contact our sales team at sales@flourishsen.com or call +234 802 301 1230 for custom pricing on large orders.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank Transfer: All major Nigerian banks supported

Pay on Delivery: Available in Lagos, Abuja, Rivers, Asaba, Akure, and Benin

Mobile Money: Coming soon!

Card Payments: Coming soon!

How does bank transfer work?

1. Complete your order and select "Bank Transfer"

2. You'll receive our bank details and a unique reference number

3. Transfer the exact amount using the reference number

4. Upload your payment receipt for verification

5. We process your order within 1 hour after confirmation

Is pay on delivery safe?

Yes! Our delivery agents carry proper ID and you can inspect items before payment. You can reject damaged or incorrect items. Pay only when you're satisfied with your order.

What if my payment fails?

If your bank transfer fails or is delayed, contact us immediately. We'll hold your order for 24 hours. For pay on delivery, there's no risk of payment failure.

Can I get a receipt?

Yes! You'll receive an email receipt after payment confirmation. For pay on delivery, our agent will provide a printed receipt upon payment.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Lagos & Abuja: 3-4 business days

Major Cities: 3-4 business days (Port Harcourt, Ibadan)

Other States: 3-4 business days

Remote Areas: 3-4 business days

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes! We deliver to all 36 states in Nigeria plus FCT. Some remote areas may have extended delivery times and additional charges. Contact us to confirm delivery to your specific location.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! After your order ships, You can track it on our website or contact our support team for updates.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

Our delivery agent will call you before arrival. If you're not available, we can reschedule delivery for a convenient time or deliver to an alternative address you specify.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Yes! We deliver Monday through Saturday. Sunday deliveries are not available.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, but only within 24 hours of placing your order and before it ships. Contact our support team immediately to make changes. Once shipped, address changes are not possible.

What is your return policy?

Return Window: 1 week from delivery date

Condition: Items must be unworn with original tags

How do I return an item?

2. Provide your order number and reason for return

3. We'll provide a return shipping label

4. Package the item securely with original tags

5. Drop off at designated pickup point or schedule pickup

Can I exchange instead of return?

What if I received a damaged item?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for immediate replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs. Damaged items can be returned beyond the 30-day window.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days within Nigeria. Lagos and Abuja orders usually arrive within 2-3 days, while other states may take 3-5 days.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can change your delivery address within 24 hours of placing your order. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.

What if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, please contact us. We'll track your order and provide updates or arrange for expedited delivery if needed.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Yes, we deliver Monday through Saturday. Sunday deliveries are available in Lagos and Abuja for urgent orders with additional charges.

How do I track my order without an order number?

Contact our support team with your phone number and email address used during checkout. We can help you locate your order and provide tracking information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and offer pay-on-delivery for most locations across Nigeria. All major Nigerian banks are supported for transfers.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if payment hasn't been processed. After processing, cancellation may incur charges depending on the order status.

What if I receive a damaged item?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damaged item. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund within 24 hours of reporting the issue.
