30-Day Return Policy

At FlourishSen, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unworn and in original condition
  • All original tags must be attached
  • Items must be returned in original packaging
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Custom or personalized items cannot be returned

How to Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at +234 801 234 5678
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization and shipping label
  4. Package items securely and attach return label
  5. Drop off at designated courier location

Return Shipping

Refund Policy

Refund Guidelines

FlourishSen is committed to providing excellent customer service. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent for all customers.

Eligible Refunds

  • Items returned within 30 days in original condition
  • Defective or damaged items upon arrival
  • Items significantly different from description
  • Cancelled orders before shipping

Refund Process

  1. Return authorization must be obtained
  2. Items must pass quality inspection
  3. Refund processed within 5-7 business days
  4. Refund issued to original payment method

Refund Timeline

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for items that are returned in used condition, missing parts, or damaged due to customer handling.
